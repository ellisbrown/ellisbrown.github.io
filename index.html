I am a Software Engineer with experience in Research and Open Source development. I am interested in Artificial Intelligence and practical applications of Deep Learning (especially in R&D).

I was the lead researcher all Spring on a NSF funded project at the intersection of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence. Since graduation, I have continued work on the project, and am a lead author on our paper being submitted to AAAI-18 entitled "A Computational Cognitive Architecture to Model Human Visual Search for Spatiotemporal Targets." In addition, I will give an oral presentation of my research at the AISES National Conference in late September.

I graduated from Vanderbilt University in May 2017 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics.
